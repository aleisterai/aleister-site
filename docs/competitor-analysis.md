## Strategic Takeaways

1. **Schema advantage:** thealeister.com has stronger structured data than most direct competitors. Maintain this lead — add Product schema to service pages when product matures.

2. **Content gap is the primary weakness:** New site with low content volume. Direct competitors have 3–12 months of blog content. Prioritize the keyword gaps above for Q2 2026 content sprint.

3. **SEO competitor content is too broad:** HubSpot, Zapier, and Neil Patel cover "AI for business" generically. Opportunity to rank for highly specific, founder-intent queries they won't write.

4. **"Lead arbitrage" is an unclaimed keyword cluster:** No competitor owns this space. Build 3–5 pieces of content around it before others notice.

5. **Comparison pages:** Once DA reaches ~20+, build `/alternatives/` pages targeting "Artisan AI alternative", "11x.ai alternative", and "Clay alternative" — these convert well.

6. **CWV parity:** Targeting a passing CWV score puts thealeister.com ahead of Relevance AI on technical SEO. Protect this advantage as the site scales.
