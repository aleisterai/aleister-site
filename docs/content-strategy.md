# Content Strategy — thealeister.com

## Overview

**Site:** thealeister.com
**Brand:** Aleister — AI agent for autonomous business operations
**Core Offering:** AI agents that handle lead generation, arbitrage, and business automation
**Target Audience:** Founders, solopreneurs, growth operators who want to automate revenue operations

## Keyword Research Outline

### Research Methodology
1. Seed keywords from core service pages (`/lead-arbitrage`, `/get`)
2. Expand via Google Search Console queries, competitor gap analysis
3. Prioritize: high commercial intent > informational > navigational
4. Filter: ≥ 100 monthly searches, KD < 50 preferred for new site

### Primary Seed Keywords
- AI agent for business
- autonomous lead generation
- AI lead arbitrage
- AI business automation
- autonomous business operations
- AI agent workflows
- automated lead generation system
- AI-powered sales agent

### Keyword Modifiers to Layer
- `how to` / `best` / `top` / `vs` / `tools` / `software` / `platform`
- Industry verticals: e-commerce, SaaS, agencies, real estate, insurance
- Intent: `build`, `automate`, `scale`, `replace`, `hire`

## On-Page SEO Checklist (per post)

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, meta title (≤ 60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description ≤ 160 chars with keyword + CTA
- [ ] At least one H2 contains secondary keyword
- [ ] Internal links: 2–3 to cluster pages, 1 to `/get` or `/lead-arbitrage`
- [ ] FAQPage JSON-LD for question-format posts
- [ ] BreadcrumbList schema (`Home > Blog > Post Title`)
- [ ] OpenGraph image (1200×630) with post title
- [ ] Canonical URL set
- [ ] Word count: 1,200+ for cluster pages, 2,500+ for pillar pages
