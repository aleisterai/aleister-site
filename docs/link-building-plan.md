## HARO (Help a Reporter Out) Setup

HARO connects journalists with expert sources. Earning a quote generates a high-authority backlink.

### Signup Steps
1. Go to [helpareporter.com](https://www.helpareporter.com) (or the current Cision/Connectively equivalent)
2. Sign up as a **Source** (not a reporter)
3. Select categories: **Business & Finance**, **Insurance**, **Personal Finance**
4. Set email digest frequency: **3x daily** (morning, afternoon, evening)
5. Set up a Gmail filter so HARO emails land in a dedicated label

### Response Best Practices
- Respond within **1–2 hours** of the digest arriving — speed matters
- Keep pitches under 200 words; lead with your credential
- Include your name, title, company URL, and a direct quote they can use as-is
- Use a consistent sign-off: "Aleister [Last Name], [Title], [site URL]"
- Track pitches in a simple spreadsheet: date, outlet, query topic, sent/not sent, result

### Sample Pitch Template
```
Hi [Reporter Name],

[One sentence credential]. [2–3 sentence answer to their specific question, quote-ready.]

Happy to provide more detail or a follow-up quote.

— Aleister [Last Name]
[Title] | [site URL]
[phone optional]
```

## Review Collection Templates

### Email Template

**Subject:** Quick favor — can you share your experience?

```
Hi [First Name],

Thank you for working with us — it was great helping you [brief outcome, e.g., "find better coverage"].

If you have 60 seconds, a quick review would mean a lot to us and helps other people find us when they need help too.

[Leave a Google Review →] (link to Google review shortcut)

If anything wasn't perfect, just reply to this email — I'd love to make it right.

Thanks again,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Phone]
```

**Send timing:** 24–48 hours after a successful interaction closes.

---

### SMS Template

```
Hi [First Name], it's [Name] from [Company]. Thanks for trusting us!
If you have a moment, a Google review helps others find us: [short link]
Reply STOP to opt out.
```

**Send timing:** Same day as interaction closes, during business hours (9am–6pm local).

---

### Review Request Guidelines
- Ask once via email, once via SMS (if opted in) — never more
- Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google/Yelp TOS)
- Always include an opt-out / "reply if unhappy" escape valve
- Aim for Google first, then Yelp, then BBB
- Respond publicly to every review within 48 hours (positive and negative)
