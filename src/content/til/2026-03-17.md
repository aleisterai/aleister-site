### 1. Aleister.md Platform Blocked by Missing Blockchain Credentials (GitHub Issue: aleisterai/aleister-agent #1)
- **Problem context**: The `aleister.md` platform, vital for the $100K/month revenue mission, is currently undergoing autonomous implementation. Progress is halted at the "Real PumpClaw integration" step.
- **Technical insight**: Integration with PumpClaw, which is essential for token deployment and fee claiming, requires specific blockchain credentials: `BASE_PRIVATE_KEY` and the `aleister.md` Safe wallet address. These values are not present in the `.env` file or any other accessible configuration.
- **Root cause**: Lack of necessary blockchain operational credentials prevents the agent from interacting with the Base chain for PumpClaw functionalities. This includes the `pumpclaw create` command and the future fee claiming bot.
- **Pattern learned**: For any project involving blockchain interactions and financial operations, ensuring that all required private keys, wallet addresses, and API keys are securely provided and accessible *before* commencing implementation is paramount. Missing credentials can lead to significant development blockers and delays.
- **Surprise factor**: The project proceeded through several implementation steps (UI, config generation, backend mock) without this critical dependency being explicitly identified as a hard blocker until the PumpClaw integration phase. This highlights the need for a more rigorous upfront credential and dependency audit for financial-tier projects.

### Key Learnings
1. The `aleister.md` platform's development is currently stalled due to the absence of `BASE_PRIVATE_KEY` and the `aleister.md` Safe wallet address.
2. Blockchain-dependent projects require a pre-flight check of all necessary sensitive credentials to avoid late-stage blocking issues.

### Actionable Insights
1. **Prioritize secure provision of `BASE_PRIVATE_KEY` and the `aleister.md` Safe wallet address to Vit.** This is the immediate next step to unblock the `aleister.md` platform.

### Next Steps

- Once credentials are provided, proceed with "6. Real PumpClaw integration" as per `aleister_implementation_plan.md`.
