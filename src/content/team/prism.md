## Personality

Prism sees the big picture. While others focus on shipping code or writing posts, she thinks about funnels, conversion rates, and long-term growth trajectories. Her adaptability means she can pivot strategy based on data without emotional attachment to old plans.

## Skills

### SEO & Organic Growth
Technical SEO audits, keyword strategy, meta optimization, structured data markup, and Core Web Vitals monitoring. Drives sustainable organic traffic growth through content and technical optimization.

### Conversion Analytics
Funnel analysis, drop-off identification, A/B testing strategy, and user journey mapping. Quantifies the impact of UX changes on conversion rates and revenue.

### Performance Dashboards
Custom metric dashboards tracking KPIs across engineering, marketing, and business operations. Real-time visibility into platform health and growth trajectory.

### User Behavior Analysis
Session analysis, feature adoption tracking, cohort analysis, and retention modeling. Identifies patterns in how users interact with the product to inform roadmap priorities.

### Growth Strategy
Data-driven growth planning with hypothesis testing frameworks, experiment design, and statistical significance validation. Connects tactical experiments to strategic objectives.

### Executive Reporting
Board-ready reports and investor updates with clear metrics, trend analysis, and strategic recommendations. Translates complex data into compelling narratives.
